About OpenDomain.name

A fast, honest domain search tool — no logins required, no upsells, no noise.

What it is

OpenDomain.name is a domain name search tool that helps you find available domains quickly. Type a word or phrase, and it instantly generates name ideas across popular TLDs — then checks each one for availability in real time.

Unlike most domain search tools, OpenDomain.name doesn't bury results under ads, upsell you on add-ons at checkout, or inflate prices. It just tells you what's open.

How the search works

Most domain search tools rely on paid third-party APIs to check availability. OpenDomain.name takes a different approach: it queries Cloudflare's public DNS over HTTPS service directly from your browser.

Every registered domain has nameserver (NS) records in the global DNS. If a domain returns NXDOMAIN — meaning no DNS record exists — it hasn't been registered. No API key needed, no per-query cost, no middleman.

Suggestions are generated locally using a lightweight JavaScript algorithm that combines your keyword with common prefixes, suffixes, and TLD variants. It runs instantly, with zero network calls.

Why it's free

OpenDomain.name earns a small commission when you purchase a domain through one of the registrar links. The price you pay is exactly the same — the registrar shares a portion of their margin with us for the referral. This keeps the tool free for everyone with no subscriptions, no ads, and no data selling.

Who made it

OpenDomain.name was built by Darrell, a designer and developer who wanted a cleaner way to search for domains. If you have feedback or ideas, feel free to reach out.

Accuracy

DNS-based availability checks are highly accurate for most domains. Occasionally a domain may show as available when it's in a redemption period, or show as taken when DNS hasn't fully propagated. Always verify at the registrar before purchasing.
