Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

OpenDomain.name uses cookies only where strictly necessary to provide the service. We do not use tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies.

What cookies we use

Cookie Purpose Duration
sb-* Supabase session — keeps you signed in Until sign out or expiry

That's it. No Google Analytics, no advertising pixels, no third-party trackers.

What cookies we don't use

We do not set cookies for advertising, remarketing, user profiling, or analytics. We do not share cookie data with any third parties for marketing purposes.

Session cookies explained

The sb-* cookie is set by Supabase, our authentication provider, when you sign in. It stores an encrypted session token that identifies your account. Without this cookie, you'd be signed out on every page load. If you don't sign in, this cookie is never set.

How to control cookies

You can block or delete cookies through your browser settings. Blocking the Supabase session cookie will prevent sign-in functionality, but OpenDomain.name's core search features work without any cookies at all.
