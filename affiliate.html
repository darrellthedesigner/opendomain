Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: March 18, 2026

OpenDomain.name is free to use. To keep it that way, we participate in affiliate programs with domain registrars. This page explains how that works.

How affiliate links work

When you click a "grab it" button on OpenDomain.name, you're taken to a domain registrar's website. That link contains a referral code identifying OpenDomain.name as the source. If you purchase a domain within the registrar's attribution window, we earn a small commission.

This commission comes from the registrar's margin — it does not increase the price you pay. The price shown at the registrar is the same whether you arrive via OpenDomain.name or navigate there directly.

Our editorial independence

The registrars shown on OpenDomain.name were selected based on price, reliability, and user experience — not commission rates. We show all of them together so you can compare and choose the best option for you.

We do not accept payment from registrars to be featured, and we do not rank or hide results based on affiliate relationships.
